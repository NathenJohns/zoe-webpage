What Makes Me Unique?

"I’ve always thought that there is nothing more important than love, that everything you do should be done with love; especially planning your wedding. People tend to get lost in the details and forget what’s important. I believe taking away the pressure of planning, helping people focus on each other and why they are getting married is a job worth doing.

"Being trusted to manage the worry and confusion, commissioned to arrange the details and keep the couple excited and expectant, makes the job worthwhile. Knowing that your hard work is appreciated and seeing the happiness it brings to others is priceless, and why I started this business; so that every wedding is planned and accomplished with faith, hope and love."

How Can I Be of Help?

Being able to tailor your wedding to your budget and needs is key. In order to achieve this I have created several different services available for you.

Full Package

The Full Package is the best option to take away the stress of planning from the start. This package can be edited to fit each couple and engage the services of a wedding planner to the full.

This package includes:

  • Budget Planning
  • Vision/Theme Discussion
  • Venue Sourcing
  • Vendor Sorting
  • Paperwork Help
  • Timeline & Checklist
  • Oversee Venue Setup
  • Wedding Day Attendance

Part Package

The Part Package is good for those who have already started planning; but would like or need an extra hand pulling together the details you've arranged, and the ones that need tying in. This package can be personalised to make sure you get the wedding day you've been wishing for.

This package includes:

  • Vendor Sourcing/Organising
  • Payment Co-ordination
  • Timeline & Checklist
  • Organisation of 'To Do List'
  • *Extra option - Wedding Day Attendance

Day of Package

Sit back and relax, knowing that there is someone on hand to take care of everything on your special day. Establishing contact with your chosen vendors and venue, familiarising myself with your wishes and vision of how you want your day to proceed, will be done in advance; allowing the stressful details of the day to be dealt with for you.

This package includes:

  • Prior Contact with Vendors/Venue
  • Confirmation of Scheduling
  • Timeline
  • Service Alignment
  • First Point of Contact on the Day
